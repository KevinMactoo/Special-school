Who We are

iEARN(International Education and Resource Network) is an organization in more than 140 countries. iEARN empowers educators and young people to work together online using the Internet and other new technologies.Over 2,000,000 young people each day are engaged in collaborative project work worldwide.

At Code Club, we think all children should have the opportunity to learn to code, no matter who they are or where they come from.

A safe and structured environment in which young people can communicate and learn new skills or finesse the existing ones.

An opportunity to apply knowledge in service-learning projects based on the UN sustainable Development Goals.

A community of experts, educators and learners making a difference as part of the educational process and ensuring 21st century success

Join Us

we run Code Clubs and Bootcamps. We work with our educators and partners to run free coding clubs where 9 to 13-year-olds build and share their ideas. We believe in learning through creation, and our coding projects offer young people plenty of opportunities to be creative and to share their creations with each other.We are part of the Raspberry Pi Foundation. Bootcamps we organize educational events that allow children to create projects based on their talent and interest and have fun by learning, coding and building technology.
